# Rust Code of Conduct

A condensed version of the [Rust Code of Conduct](https://www.rust-lang.org/policies/code-of-conduct).

## Standards for Behavior

- We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of level of experience, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.
- Please be kind and courteous. There's no need to be mean or rude.
- Respect that people have differences of opinion and that every design or implementation choice carries a trade-off and numerous costs. There is seldom a single right answer to any problem.
- Please keep exclusionary jokes to a minimum. In particular, exclusionary jokes based on race or gender are not acceptable.
- Harassment and other exclusionary behavior aren't acceptable. This includes, but is not limited to:
  - Personal insults, particularly those related to gender, sexual orientation, race, religion, or disability.
  - Inappropriate sexual attention or imagery.
  - Gratuitous or off-topic sexual images or behaviour in spaces where they're not appropriate.
  - Physical violence or threats of violence.
